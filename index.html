About JayTree LLC

JayTree LLC is a 100% veteran-owned business specializing in sustainable forestry mulching and land clearing services across Central Texas. Using state-of-the-art compact track loaders with high-flow mulchers and brush cutters, we transform overgrown land into usable space while enriching the soil and minimizing environmental impact—no burning, no hauling debris.

Proudly Veteran-Owned • Serving Wiliamson County and Surrounding Areas

Our Services

Forestry Mulching

Grind trees, brush, and undergrowth into nutrient-rich mulch on-site. Perfect for wildfire mitigation, trail creation, and property prep.

Brush & Land Clearing

Efficient removal of dense vegetation for building sites, fence lines, pastures, and right-of-ways using industrial attachments.

Custom Projects

From small residential lots to larger commercial/agricultural sites—we handle it all with precision and care.
